Suffolk Construction

Suffolk construction is a $5billion dollar construction contractor based out of Boston, Ma. I was hired to assist the innovation director in developing a frontend architecture for a new project management platform.

This project is still under development, and I can't talk about most of what I did while employed there. But it was at the cutting edge of real-time collaboration software and brought those capabilities to the construction plan and control sector.

The Front-End stack that I worked with involved Bryntum.js, HTML and CSS, Express.js, Node.js, Postgres and Playwright end to end testing.
